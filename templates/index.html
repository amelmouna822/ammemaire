About the Website

Welcome to our smart platform for analyzing and filtering résumés!
In today's recruitment world, many companies struggle with sorting
through thousands of résumés to select the most suitable candidates.
On the other hand, job seekers often find it difficult to access
opportunities that match their skills and experiences.
That's where the idea for this website came from.
This platform offers an innovative solution that combines
artificial intelligence and modern web technologies to assist both companies and job seekers.

How Does It Work?

The website relies on Natural Language Processing (NLP)
techniques to analyze résumé content, extracting skills,
qualifications, and years of experience.
This information is then matched with job offer requirements,
and the system calculates a compatibility score and displays the results clearly.

Our Goal:

We aim to improve the hiring experience for both sides by:
Speeding up the recruitment process and reducing manual effort.
Ensuring better alignment between job roles and suitable candidates.
Promoting fairness in hiring through objective, data-driven analysi.
